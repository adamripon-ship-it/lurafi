# About Mitipi

> The Swiss company behind KEVIN 3.0, its ownership model, the markets it serves, and how to get in touch.

## Company

**Mitipi** is a **Swiss** company and the maker of **KEVIN 3.0**, a home-security presence simulator and burglar deterrent. Mitipi's approach centres on **prevention rather than reaction**: instead of recording or responding to intruders, its products deter break-ins by making a property look and sound occupied.

## Product line

Mitipi's product is **KEVIN 3.0** ("Kevin"), a Swiss-engineered device that simulates a believable human presence using patented light and shadow effects and authentic pre-recorded household sound. Kevin uses no camera and no microphone, stores and plays its simulations locally, keeps working even if Wi-Fi is jammed, and doubles as a Bluetooth speaker and lamp when you are home. An independent review of the third-generation Kevin has been published by **Galaxus** (https://www.galaxus.ch).

## Ownership model

Kevin is designed to be owned outright, with no ongoing service fees:

- **One-time purchase** — no monitoring subscription.
- **Lifetime software updates** — the device continues to receive software updates.
- **3-year Swiss warranty**.
- **Free delivery across Europe**.
